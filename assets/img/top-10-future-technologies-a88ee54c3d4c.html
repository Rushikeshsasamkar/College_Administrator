Top 10 Future Technologies

Originally published by Ajay Kapoor onMarch 21st 2019

@kapoorajay081Ajay Kapoor

Hey! I am a software consultant working in an MNC. I like giving software advises to startups & SMEs

Can anyone today sit and predict whats going to happen in the future? Can business enthusiasts and entrepreneurs really think clearly about and predict the future of technology?

Are we going to need the human touch in the future or will this be replaced with the help of the machines and robots? These are some of the confusing questions that you will find answers to in thisblog.

Technology seems to be constantly evolving and for worse or better, is making its path towards speed, sophistication, and ease each year. For many years, technology advances at a fast pace and that does not seem to be slowing or stopping down any timesoon.

There are many technologies that have already set their benchmark in the industry and only a few more applications are away from becoming the mainstream.

These are some of the future technologies that are going to become an incredible future in the coming years. So, without wasting much of your time, lets start listing out these technologies one byone.

Future Technology 1: The Internet ofThings:

Since its inception, the top-most IOT development companies have been using this technology to improve productivity, inventory tracking, management, and security processes. It is estimated that by the year 2020, almost a quarter of billion cares will get hooked by the Internet, making them evensmarter.

The IoT technology is eventually getting its pace and, as many of the reports suggest, in the near future, we are going to see IoT devices becoming more and more popular, for many general purpose computing tasks.

Future Technology 2: Blockchain Technology:

Blockchain, the brainchild of an individual or group of individuals known by the pseudonym, Satoshi Nakamoto, has applications reaching far beyond the financial transactions.

Companies have applied the blockchain technology to almost everything, from simplifying tracking & access to information in academia to amusing and interesting games that use complex algorithms to create some unique experiences. While pervasive in only a handful of industries right now, by the year 2020 the world is going to see its huge adoption.

Future Technology 3: Artificial Intelligence

The above image shows how Artificial Intelligence will take over the world in the next few years. This technology seems to be making its place in the present as well as future technology due to the advanced technical changes it has been making worldwide.

The everyday tasks of Artificial Intelligence of enabling the customers to see through facial recognition, read the messages and reports, speak, and record the emotions through effective computing will help AI become an independent technology that no longer needs human assistance to learn anything.

Future Technology 4.NLP:

As the NLP technology evolves over years, computers will be extending their present capabilities from processing to understanding human language in quite a holisticway.

Until now, NLP is restricted to infer only a limited range of human emotions including the feelings of anger or joy. Eventually, NLP will be programmed in such a way that it understands more complex elements of human emotions such as sarcasm, humor, irony, cynicism, andsatire.

Future Technology 5. Facial Recognition:

Your face is going to become your technology partner by the year 2020. This technology came into light when the iPhoneX was launched with one of the best features- FacialID.

In the future, with the help of this technology, the software development companies will be using this technology for developing a series of applications. By 2020, you will be able to unlock your house, car, etc., with the help of facial technology.

Future Technology 6. Automation:

Along with Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Robots, and Deep Learning, automation has moved quite ahead in the production lines. From packing the product boxes to delivering the items at the doors of the customers, automation seems to be one of the most awaited technologies of the year2020.

Food, medical, beverage & customer service will also become further automated and streamlined by the time 2020hits.

Future Technology 7. Cognitive Technology

The cognitive technology umbrella comprises of things like speech recognition and natural language processing (NLP). Combined, these various technologies are able to optimize and automate a lot of tasks that were previously done by individuals, including certain aspects of analytics and accounting.

Even though cognitive technologies have a wide range of applications, it is predicted that the industry sector most affected by this technology initially will be the software sector with about 93% of the software development companies are projected to adopt these technologies by2020.

Future Technology 8. Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality:

A few of the many industry sectors that are taking advantage of AR/VR technologies and exploring all options are the manufacturing healthcare and transport industry. The VR headsets today, are difficult to use since they require sensors and cables attached to a computer, however, these devices, too, are evolving, with the help of the mobile and wirelessdevices.

This evolution will allow businesses to build AR/VR features into larger & more holistic applications, creating a complete set of capabilities for more meaningful customer experiences.

Future Technology 9. Prescriptive Analysis:

The digital technology in the year 2020, along with giving the businesses the data related to how customers will react, will also guide them on how they shall act when the customer behaves in a certain way. What was once only a source of insights will now become a complete guide of the next profitable move.

Future Technology 10. Edge Computing:

Edge computing is a kind of computing that is done at or near the source of the data, instead of relying on the cloud at one of the many data centers to do all the necessary work.

Even though things move instantly to and from the cloud, there are still some issues when data hops around the world. For such reasons, edge computing will mean faster response times for many of the internet-connected devices.

Conclusion:

The above-mentioned are the top 10 technologies that are going to take over the industry in the upcoming years. In order to work on these technologies and develop great and innovative applications, you can take help from an IT consulting company that has all the expertise in these technologies. Hire a software development company that has experienced and qualified professionals who can work smartly and creatively in these technologies and help you reshape your business in the most innovative and intelligent way.
